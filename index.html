How does it work?

1.

Register

Set up your company in Happyforce dashboard defining your departamental structure and employee attributes (i.e. gender, age, etc.)

Invite your employees using their corporate email account

Employees receive an invitation mail with a code they have to use to anonymously activate their apps

2.

Vote

Through the smartphone apps employees share their mood and comments on a daily basis

After voting employees can see the Happiness Index and the rest of the comments in real time

Employees can rate any comment they like or dislike

3.

Analyse

Happyforce turns employee feedback into graphical data easy to read by managers and HR

Use Happyforce Dashboard to observe your team’s happiness evolution over time

Process and analyse direct feedback from anonymous comments of your team in real time.
